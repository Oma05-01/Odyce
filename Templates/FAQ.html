How can we help?

Everything you need to know about the Odyce experience.

What is Odyce Perfume?

Odyce is a luxury fragrance house dedicated to artisanal scent creation. Our collection is curated to suit diverse personalities, utilizing premium oils to ensure longevity and a unique sillage.

How do I place an order?

Browse our digital gallery and select your desired fragrance. Once on the product page, click "Buy Now". For distributors, please ensure you are logged in to access bulk pricing.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, Odyce ships globally. We utilize premium couriers to ensure your scent arrives safely. Delivery times and fees vary based on your international location.

What is your return policy?

Due to the personal nature of fragrances, we accept returns within 30 days only if the security seal is intact and the product is in its original, unopened packaging.
